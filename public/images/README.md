# Brick images for Bricks Transport website

**Current images in use:**
- `hero-bricks-only.jpg` – Brick stacks and kiln (no vehicle), hero section
- `blue-metal.jpg` – Blue metal aggregate, products section
- `m-sand.jpg`, `p-sand.jpg` – M sand and P sand, products section
- `m-sand.jpg` – M sand (manufactured sand), M sand & P sand section
- `p-sand.jpg` – P sand (plastering sand), M sand & P sand section
- `brick-clear-1.jpg` – Wire-cut brick clear finish (single), about section
- `brick-clear-2.jpg` – Stacked red bricks clear finish, about section
- `brick-clear-3.jpg` – Red clay bricks clear finish, about section

**Brick sizes** shown on the site are based on [Wire Cut Brick in Chennai – IndiaMART](https://dir.indiamart.com/chennai/wire-cut-brick.html) (9×4×3 in., 190×90×90 mm, 12×6×4 in., etc.). Replace these image files here to update the site; keep the same names or update the app templates.
